<p><p>The radial fan market encompasses various types, including paddle wheel, open wheel, and back plate wheel designs. Open wheel fans are designed for greater flexibility with a less obstructive airflow pattern, making them suitable for cooling applications. Back plate wheel fans typically feature a housing that enhances performance by minimizing turbulence, making them effective for medium to high-pressure applications across various industries.</p></p>
