<p><p>The photo laminators market is primarily divided into two types: pouch laminators and roll laminators. Pouch laminators are compact devices that use pre-cut, sealed pouches to laminate individual documents, making them ideal for home or small office use. Roll laminators, on the other hand, use two large rolls of laminating film to laminate wider materials, such as posters or banners, and are suited for larger commercial applications. Each type caters to different needs based on volume and material size.</p></p>
