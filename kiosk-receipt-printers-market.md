<p><ul><li>UHF</li><li>HF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Kiosk receipt printers operate primarily in two market types: UHF (Ultra High Frequency) and HF (High Frequency). UHF printers are designed for long-range communication, typically used in environments like retail or logistics, where efficient data transfer and scanning are crucial. In contrast, HF printers are suited for close-range applications, often found in public transport or payment kiosks, ensuring quick transactions. Both types cater to specific needs based on their operational range and application settings.</p></p>
