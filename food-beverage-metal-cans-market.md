<p><p>The Food & Beverage Metal Cans market comprises primarily two types: three-piece cans and two-piece cans. Three-piece cans consist of a separate body and two ends, typically welded or soldered together, offering versatility in shape and size for various products. In contrast, two-piece cans are formed from a single sheet of metal, with the bottom and sides combined, resulting in a smoother design and lighter weight. Both types cater to different packaging needs, maintaining product integrity and enhancing shelf life.</p></p>
