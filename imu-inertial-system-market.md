Low accuracy IMUs are cost-effective and suitable for applications like consumer electronics, providing basic motion sensing. Medium precision IMUs offer a balance between performance and price, often used in drones and robotics, where moderate accuracy is sufficient. High precision IMUs are designed for demanding applications, such as aerospace and defense, delivering advanced motion tracking and stability with minimal errors, ensuring optimal operational reliability.</p></p>
