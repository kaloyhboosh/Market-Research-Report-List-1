<p><p>The Micro LED Mass Transfer market comprises several key methods for efficiently placing micro LEDs onto substrates. </p><p>1. **Electrostatic Power Adsorption** utilizes electric fields to attract micro LEDs onto surfaces. </p><p>2. **Liquid Assembly Transfer** employs a liquid medium to facilitate the movement and placement of micro LEDs. </p><p>3. **Elastomeric Print Film Transfer** uses flexible materials to pick and place micro LEDs accurately. </p><p>4. **Laser Transfer** leverages focused laser beams to selectively position micro LEDs, ensuring precision and reducing damage.</p></p>
