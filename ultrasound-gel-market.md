<p><strong>The Ultrasound Gel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sterile</li><li>Non-Sterile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrasound gel market is divided into sterile and non-sterile types, each serving distinct purposes. Sterile ultrasound gel is used in clinical settings, ensuring a contamination-free environment for procedures such as ultrasounds, biopsies, and catheterizations. It is typically packaged in single-use containers. In contrast, non-sterile ultrasound gel is suitable for general use in a variety of non-invasive procedures, such as routine imaging, and is often available in larger containers for multiple usage. Both types are essential for optimal ultrasound conductivity and patient safety.</p></p>
