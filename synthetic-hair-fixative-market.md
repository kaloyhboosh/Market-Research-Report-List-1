<p><p>The synthetic hair fixative market is primarily categorized into two types: non-ionic polymers and ionic polymers. Non-ionic polymers operate without charge, providing a flexible hold and minimizing build-up on hair. They are ideal for applications requiring a natural finish. In contrast, ionic polymers carry a positive or negative charge, offering strong adhesion and durability. This type is suitable for long-lasting effects, often used in professional hair products. Both types serve diverse consumer needs in styling and hair care.</p></p>
