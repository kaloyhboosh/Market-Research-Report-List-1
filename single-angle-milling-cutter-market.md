<p><ul><li>45 Degree Angles</li><li>60 Degree Angles</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single-angle milling cutter market includes two primary types: 45-degree and 60-degree angles. The 45-degree angle cutters are commonly used for creating grooves and bevels, offering efficient chip removal and improved surface finish. In contrast, the 60-degree angle cutters are ideal for making V-shaped grooves and intricate profiles, providing better accuracy in angular cuts. Both types play a crucial role in various manufacturing processes, catering to different machining needs in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and metalworking.</p></p>
