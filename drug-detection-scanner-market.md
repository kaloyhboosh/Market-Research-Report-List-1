<p><p>The drug detection scanner market comprises several technologies, including Gamma-Ray Radiography, X-Ray Radiography, and Muon Tomography. Gamma-Ray Radiography utilizes high-energy gamma rays to detect substances based on their radioactive signatures, offering high penetration depth. X-Ray Radiography employs X-ray imaging to identify concealed drugs through density and material contrast. Muon Tomography leverages cosmic muons to analyze dense materials and detect anomalies, providing a natural, non-invasive method for scanning. Each technology offers unique advantages in effectively identifying illicit substances.</p></p>
