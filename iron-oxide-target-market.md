<p><p>The iron oxide target market is divided into two main types: plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets are flat, stationary disks used in various applications such as sputtering processes for thin film deposition. They provide a uniform coating but have limitations in material utilization. Rotating targets, on the other hand, are cylindrical and continuously rotate during operation, allowing for better utilization of the material and improved deposition rates. This distinction caters to different industrial needs and enhances efficiency in production processes.</p></p>
