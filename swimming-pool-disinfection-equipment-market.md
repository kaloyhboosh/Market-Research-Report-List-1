<p><p>Swimming pool disinfection equipment encompasses various types designed to maintain water quality and safety. Chlorine dosing equipment adds chlorine to eliminate pathogens. Ozonation disinfection equipment utilizes ozone gas to disinfect water effectively, reducing chemical use. Ultraviolet (UV) disinfection equipment employs UV light to neutralize microorganisms without chemicals. Electrolysis disinfection equipment generates chlorine through the electrolysis of saltwater, providing a more natural disinfection method. Other equipment may include alternatives like mineral systems, each offering unique benefits for pool maintenance.</p></p>
