<p><strong>The Weld Cleaner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MIG Weld Cleaning</li><li>TIG Weld Cleaning</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Weld Cleaner market is primarily categorized into two types: MIG (Metal Inert Gas) weld cleaning and TIG (Tungsten Inert Gas) weld cleaning. MIG weld cleaning involves the removal of oxidation and contaminants from MIG welds, often using chemicals or electrochemical processes to ensure a smooth finish. TIG weld cleaning, on the other hand, focuses on the precise cleaning of stainless steel and other metals after TIG welding, utilizing specialized electrolytic solutions for effective decontamination and surface preparation. Both techniques enhance weld quality and aesthetics.</p></p>
