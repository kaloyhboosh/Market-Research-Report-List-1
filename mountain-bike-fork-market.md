<p><p>The mountain bike fork market primarily features two types of suspension systems: air spring and coil spring forks. Air spring suspension forks utilize compressed air to adjust the fork's stiffness and rebound characteristics, offering lightweight performance and tunability. In contrast, coil spring suspension forks use metal springs for absorbing shocks, providing a more consistent feel and better durability in rugged terrains. Both types cater to different rider preferences and styles, influencing weight, maintenance, and ride quality in mountain biking experiences.</p></p>
