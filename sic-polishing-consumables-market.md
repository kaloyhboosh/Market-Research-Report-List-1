<p><p>The SiC polishing consumables market is primarily divided into two categories: SiC polishing slurries and SiC polishing pads. SiC polishing slurries are liquid abrasives that enhance the precision and efficiency of the polishing process, allowing for fine surface finishes on silicon carbide substrates. SiC polishing pads, on the other hand, are used as the physical medium that interacts with the substrate during polishing, providing essential support and uniformity in surface treatment. Both are critical for achieving the desired surface quality in semiconductor and electronics applications.</p></p>
