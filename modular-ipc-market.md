<p><p>Modular IPC (Industrial PC) markets consist of two primary types: Box Type and Panel Type. Box Type IPCs are standalone units, typically housed in robust enclosures, designed for flexible installations in various environments like factories or control rooms. In contrast, Panel Type IPCs are integrated into displays or touch panels, offering a compact solution for applications requiring direct user interaction, such as kiosks or operator interfaces in machinery. Both types are essential for enhancing automation and data processing in industrial settings.</p></p>
