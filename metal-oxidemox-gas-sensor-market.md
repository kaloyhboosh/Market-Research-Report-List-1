<p><p>Metal-Oxide (MOX) gas sensors are devices that utilize metal-oxide materials to detect and measure the concentration of various gases in the environment. These sensors work on the principle of changes in electrical resistance when exposed to target gases, providing rapid and sensitive detection capabilities. MOX sensors are widely used in applications such as air quality monitoring, industrial safety, automotive emissions testing, and smart home devices due to their reliability, cost-effectiveness, and ease of integration.</p><p>The Metal-Oxide (MOX) Gas Sensor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.7% during the forecast period, driven by increasing environmental concerns and regulatory pressures that necessitate air quality monitoring in urban areas.
