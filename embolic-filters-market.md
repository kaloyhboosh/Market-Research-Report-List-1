<p><strong>The Embolic Filters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Upper Chamber Embolic Filter</li><li>Inferior Chamber Embolic Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Embolic filters are medical devices designed to capture and remove debris from the bloodstream during procedures like cardiothoracic surgery or catheter-based interventions. The upper chamber embolic filter targets debris originating from the heart's upper chambers, effectively preventing complications during interventions involving the atria. In contrast, the inferior chamber embolic filter focuses on filtering material from the heart's lower chambers, specifically the ventricles. Both types enhance patient safety and outcomes by minimizing the risk of embolic events during surgical procedures.</p></p>
