<p><ul><li>Monitoring ECG Systems</li><li>Diagnostic ECG Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ECG market is primarily divided into two main types: monitoring ECG systems and diagnostic ECG systems. Monitoring ECG systems are designed for continuous observation of heart activity, typically used in hospitals and critical care settings, providing real-time data for patient management. In contrast, diagnostic ECG systems focus on capturing and analyzing the heart's electrical activity for diagnostic purposes, often used in outpatient settings for disease detection, such as arrhythmias or myocardial infarctions, ensuring appropriate patient treatment.</p></p>
