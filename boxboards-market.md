<p><ul><li>Testliner</li><li>Kraftliner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Boxboards encompass various market types, primarily Testliner and Kraftliner. Testliner is typically made from recycled paper and is used for the outer layers of corrugated boxes, offering a cost-effective solution for packaging. Kraftliner, on the other hand, is produced from virgin wood pulp, providing superior strength and durability, making it ideal for high-quality packaging needs. Both types play crucial roles in the packaging industry, catering to different requirements in terms of cost, strength, and environmental impact.</p></p>
