<p><p>Case formers are categorized into semi-automatic and fully automatic types based on their operational capabilities. Semi-automatic case formers require some manual intervention, allowing operators to perform specific tasks like loading and adjusting the machine. In contrast, fully automatic case formers streamline the packaging process, handling the entire operation autonomously from case forming to sealing, which enhances efficiency and reduces labor costs. Businesses choose between these types based on their production needs, budget, and desired automation level.</p></p>
