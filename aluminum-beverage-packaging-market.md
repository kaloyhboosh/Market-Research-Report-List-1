<p><ul><li>DRD (Draw and Redraw) Cans</li><li>DWI (Drawn and Wall Ironed) Cans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum beverage packaging market features various can types, including Draw and Redraw (DRD) cans and Drawn and Wall Ironed (DWI) cans. DRD cans are produced by drawing a sheet of aluminum into a cup and then redrawing it to achieve the final can shape, offering good strength and lightweight properties. Conversely, DWI cans are formed by drawing and then ironing the can wall to create a thinner, more uniform structure, enhancing their ability to withstand pressure, making them ideal for carbonated beverages.</p></p>
