<p><ul><li>Piston Aerators</li><li>Diaphragms Aerators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The septic aerator market consists of key types including piston aerators and diaphragm aerators. Piston aerators utilize a mechanical piston to compress air, promoting efficient oxygen transfer in septic systems, ideal for larger installations. In contrast, diaphragm aerators employ a flexible diaphragm to create air bubbles, enhancing oxygen diffusion in smaller or residential systems. Both types serve to improve wastewater treatment efficiency, supporting environmental sustainability and effective waste management in various applications.</p></p>
