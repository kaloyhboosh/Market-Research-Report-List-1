<p><p>The neurovascular embolization device market includes various types aimed at treating cerebral vascular conditions. Embolization coils are used to occlude abnormal blood vessels, primarily for aneurysm treatment. Flow diversion stents redirect blood flow away from an aneurysm, promoting healing. Liquid embolic systems involve special adhesives that seal off targeted vessels, while aneurysm clips are surgical tools employed to mechanically clamping an aneurysm neck to prevent rupture. Each device serves distinct purposes in managing neurovascular disorders, enhancing patient outcomes.</p></p>
