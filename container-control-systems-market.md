<p><p>Container Control Systems encompass various market types essential for efficient logistics. Container Acceptance involves the verification and recording of incoming containers. Container Gate-In refers to the process of registering containers as they enter a facility. Container Release is the authorization to retrieve containers from storage. Container Gate-Out manages container departures. Equipment Maintenance covers tracking and managing the condition of containers and related equipment. Billing Processing handles financial transactions, while EDI facilitates electronic data exchange. Other markets may include additional logistical services.</p></p>
