<p><p>Blood Pressure Monitoring and Measurement Instruments encompass various types, including sphygmomanometers, ambulatory and automated BP monitors, and blood pressure transducers. Sphygmomanometers, either manual or digital, measure blood pressure through cuff inflation. Ambulatory BP monitors are portable devices allowing for continuous blood pressure monitoring over 24 hours, capturing variations during daily activities. Automated BP monitors provide quick, user-friendly measurements without manual intervention. Blood pressure transducers convert pressure into electrical signals, commonly used in critical care settings for real-time monitoring.</p></p>
