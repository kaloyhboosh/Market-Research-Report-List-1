<p><p>The Passive Infrared Sensors (PIR) market mainly consists of two types: thermal and quantum sensors. Thermal PIR sensors detect infrared radiation emitted by objects based on temperature differences, making them suitable for general motion detection and security applications. Quantum PIR sensors, on the other hand, utilize advanced quantum principles to achieve higher sensitivity and accuracy, often deployed in more specialized applications like environmental monitoring and advanced imaging. Both types enhance efficiency in various sectors, including security, automation, and energy management.</p></p>
