<p><p>The flexible braided copper connectors market is segmented into two main types: flat type and round type. Flat type connectors feature a ribbon-like design, providing broad contact surfaces for improved conductivity and flexibility in tight spaces. They are often used in applications requiring low-profile configurations. In contrast, round type connectors have a cylindrical shape, suitable for applications needing robust connections and higher current handling. Both types serve essential roles in electrical systems, ensuring reliable connectivity in various industries.</p></p>
