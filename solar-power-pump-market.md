<p><p>The solar power pump market primarily consists of two types: DC solar pumps and AC solar pumps. DC solar pumps operate on direct current generated by solar panels, making them ideal for off-grid applications. They are usually more efficient for smaller systems and require less maintenance. In contrast, AC solar pumps operate on alternating current and can utilize inverters to connect to the grid or battery systems, making them suitable for larger-scale agricultural and industrial applications. Both types contribute to sustainable water pumping solutions.</p></p>
