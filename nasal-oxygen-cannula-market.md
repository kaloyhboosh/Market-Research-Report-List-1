<p><strong>The Nasal Oxygen Cannula Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-flow Nasal Oxygen Cannula</li><li>High-flow Nasal Oxygen Cannula</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nasal oxygen cannula market is segmented into low-flow and high-flow cannulas. Low-flow nasal oxygen cannulas deliver oxygen at lower rates, typically ranging from 1 to 6 liters per minute, allowing patients to breathe ambient air alongside supplemental oxygen. High-flow nasal oxygen cannulas, on the other hand, provide heated and humidified oxygen at higher rates, often exceeding 6 liters per minute, which can improve comfort and oxygenation for critically ill patients. These distinctions cater to varying patient needs in respiratory therapy.</p></p>
