<p><p>The High Throughput Screening (HTS) market encompasses several key types. Cell-based assays involve testing compounds on living cells to assess biological activity. Lab-On-A-Chip technology miniaturizes laboratory processes, enabling rapid analysis and reducing reagent usage. Ultra-High-Throughput Screening significantly increases the volume of compounds tested simultaneously. Bioinformatics utilizes computational tools to analyze complex data generated from HTS. Label-free technology detects cellular responses without the use of dyes or tags, offering a more natural assessment of compound interactions.</p></p>
