<p><p>The intensified cameras market is categorized into two main types: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) technologies. CCD intensified cameras are known for their high sensitivity and image quality, making them ideal for low-light environments and scientific applications. In contrast, CMOS intensified cameras offer advantages such as lower power consumption, faster processing speeds, and integrated functionality, making them suitable for commercial and industrial uses. Each type caters to specific needs in imaging and detection across various sectors.</p></p>
