<p><strong>The Semiconductor X-ray Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Detector</li><li>Indirect Detector</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor X-ray detector market is primarily divided into direct and indirect detectors. Direct detectors convert X-ray photons into electrical signals using semiconductor materials, offering high spatial resolution and efficiency. They are commonly used in applications like medical imaging. Indirect detectors, on the other hand, first convert X-ray photons into visible light using scintillators, which is then transformed into an electrical signal by a photodetector. Indirect detectors typically provide lower costs and are widely used in various imaging applications.</p></p>
