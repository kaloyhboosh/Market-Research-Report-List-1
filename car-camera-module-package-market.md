<p><p>The car camera module package market is segmented into several types, including image sensors, lenses, camera module assemblies, and voice coil motors (VCMs). Image sensors capture light and convert it into electronic signals, crucial for high-quality images. Lenses focus this light onto the sensor, influencing clarity and field of view. Camera module assemblies combine these components into a single unit for easier installation. VCMs adjust the lens position for autofocus, enhancing image quality and ensuring accurate capture in varying conditions.</p></p>
