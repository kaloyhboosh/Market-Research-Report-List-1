<p><p>The Fused Fiber Collimators market primarily consists of two types: Single Mode Fiber Collimators and Multimode Fiber Collimators. Single Mode Fiber Collimators are designed for long-distance communication, utilizing a single light path to minimize loss and maximize signal clarity, making them ideal for applications like telecom and data centers. In contrast, Multimode Fiber Collimators accommodate multiple light paths, allowing for higher bandwidth over shorter distances, commonly used in local area networks and short-haul communication systems.</p></p>
