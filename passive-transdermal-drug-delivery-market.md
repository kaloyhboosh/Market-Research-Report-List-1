<p><p>Passive transdermal drug delivery is a method of administering medication through the skin without the use of external energy sources. This approach relies on the natural permeability of the skin, with drugs diffusing through the stratum corneum into the systemic circulation. It offers benefits such as improved patient compliance, reduced gastrointestinal side effects, and stable drug plasma levels. </p><p>The passive transdermal drug delivery market is witnessing robust growth, driven by increasing demand for non-invasive drug administration methods and a rising prevalence of chronic diseases.
