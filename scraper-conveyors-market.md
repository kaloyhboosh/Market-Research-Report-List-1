<p><p>The scraper conveyors market includes vertical and horizontal types, each designed for efficient bulk material handling. Vertical scraper conveyors transport materials vertically using a series of scrapers attached to a chain or belt, ideal for maximizing space in facilities with limited floor area. Horizontal scraper conveyors, on the other hand, move materials along a flat or slightly inclined path, facilitating transport across longer distances. Both types are essential in industries like mining, agriculture, and manufacturing for their reliability and versatility.</p></p>
