<p><strong>The Surface Treatment Equipments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plasma Treatment Equipments</li><li>Corona Treatment Equipments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface treatment equipment includes various technologies to enhance adhesion, wettability, and surface properties of materials. Plasma treatment equipment utilizes glowing ionized gas to modify surfaces at the molecular level, improving characteristics like cleanliness and bonding. In contrast, corona treatment equipment generates a high-voltage electrical discharge to achieve surface activation, making it ideal for plastics and other non-polar substrates. Both technologies are vital in industries such as packaging, automotive, and electronics for improving product performance and longevity.</p></p>
