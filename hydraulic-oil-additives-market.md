<p><p>The hydraulic oil additives market comprises several key types, including antioxidants, anti-emulsifiers, and rust inhibitors. Antioxidants prevent the oxidation of hydraulic fluids, extending their lifespan and maintaining performance. Anti-emulsifiers help separate water from oil, preventing emulsification and ensuring optimal fluidity. Rust inhibitors protect metal surfaces from corrosion and degradation due to moisture and contaminants, enhancing the durability of hydraulic systems. Together, these additives play a crucial role in improving the efficiency and reliability of hydraulic oils.</p></p>
