<p><ul><li>Immunodiagnostics</li><li>Molecular Diagnostics</li><li>POC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MRSA testing devices market is categorized into three main types: immunodiagnostics, molecular diagnostics, and point-of-care (POC) testing. Immunodiagnostics utilize antibody-antigen reactions to detect MRSA, providing quick and reliable results. Molecular diagnostics involve techniques like PCR to identify the genetic material of MRSA, offering high sensitivity and specificity. POC testing allows for immediate results at the site of care, facilitating rapid decision-making in treatment. Together, these methods enhance the efficiency of MRSA detection and management.</p></p>
