<p><p>Diode side-pumped solid-state lasers are categorized into pulse type and continuous type based on their operation. Pulse type lasers emit energy in short bursts, making them ideal for applications requiring high peak power and precision, such as in medical procedures and material processing. Continuous type lasers produce a steady beam of light, suitable for applications needing constant power, like laser cutting and industrial welding. Both types leverage efficient diode pumping to enhance performance and provide versatile solutions across various industries.</p></p>
