<p><p>Live turning holders are specialized tool holders designed for turning operations in CNC machines. The BMT (B-axis Multi-Tasking) live turning holders facilitate simultaneous machining and enhanced precision, often utilized in multi-tasking lathes. VDI (Verein Deutscher Ingenieure) live turning holders feature a standardized design, allowing quick tool changes and compatibility across various machine types. Both types cater to diverse manufacturing needs, improving efficiency and versatility in various turning applications, thus enhancing productivity in modern machining environments.</p></p>
