<p><p>The tablet and notebook display market is primarily categorized into three types: LCD, OLED, and AMOLED displays. LCD displays utilize liquid crystal technology, offering good color accuracy and brightness at lower costs, making them widely used. OLED displays provide richer colors and deeper blacks due to self-emissive pixels, enhancing visual quality but often at a higher price. AMOLED, a variant of OLED, combines active matrix technology for better refresh rates and energy efficiency, making it popular in premium devices.</p></p>
