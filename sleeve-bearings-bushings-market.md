<p><p>Sleeve bearings, also known as bushings, are crucial components in machinery, providing low-friction support for rotating or sliding parts. The market can be divided into two primary types: radial sleeve bearings, which accommodate radial loads and facilitate rotation by allowing shafts to spin within a cylindrical sleeve; and axial sleeve bearings, designed to handle axial loads and support movement along the axis of the shaft. Each type serves specific applications, impacting performance, durability, and maintenance in various industrial sectors.</p></p>
