<p><p>The ultrafast laser market is primarily segmented into two main types: femtosecond lasers and picosecond lasers. Femtosecond lasers, which emit light pulses lasting trillionths of a second, are widely used in applications such as eye surgeries and precision material processing. Picosecond lasers, with duration in the range of quadrillionths of a second, are favored for applications requiring high-resolution engraving and medical diagnostics. Both types leverage their rapid pulse durations to achieve precise and efficient results in various industries.</p></p>
