<p><p>The multispectral camera market is primarily divided into two types: single-lens and multi-lens multispectral cameras. Single-lens multispectral cameras utilize a single optical system to capture multiple spectral bands, making them compact and cost-effective for applications like agriculture and environmental monitoring. In contrast, multi-lens multispectral cameras employ multiple lenses and sensors to capture a wider range of spectral data simultaneously, offering higher resolution and accuracy for demanding applications such as remote sensing, accurate material identification, and scientific research.</p></p>
