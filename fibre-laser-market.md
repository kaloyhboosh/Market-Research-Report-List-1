<p><strong>The Fibre Laser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fibre Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fibre Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fibre laser market is primarily divided into two types: Continuous Wave (CW) fibre lasers and Pulsed fibre lasers. CW fibre lasers emit a constant beam of light, making them ideal for applications requiring uninterrupted power, such as material cutting and welding. In contrast, Pulsed fibre lasers generate bursts of concentrated energy, which are well-suited for tasks like marking, engraving, and precision material processing. Each type offers distinct advantages depending on the specific industrial application and required performance characteristics.</p></p>
